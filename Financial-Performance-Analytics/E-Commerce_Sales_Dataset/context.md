*"The e‑commerce company has experienced fluctuating revenue over the past year. Management wants to understand the key drivers of sales performance to inform inventory planning, marketing spend, and customer retention strategies. Specifically, they need to answer:

What are the monthly and quarterly revenue trends?

Which products generate the highest revenue, and which are underperforming?

How do different payment methods contribute to total revenue?

Which customer segments (e.g., new vs. returning, high‑value vs. low‑value) drive the majority of sales?

What is the average order value (AOV), and how does it vary by product category or customer type?

The analysis will be used to recommend actionable improvements for the next quarter."*
